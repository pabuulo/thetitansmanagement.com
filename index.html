Foundation

The Titans Group Foundation fights to bring opportunities to individuals worldwide, channeling the power of the entertainment industry towards philanthropic acts. Working in several fields of need we want to promote racial equity, educational opportunities, social respect, and development.

TTG Foundation fights to put a smile on children's faces internationally. We not only fight to nurture children all around the world. But to bring them access to basic education, libraries, college experiences, and better living conditions. Our purpose is eternal, and that is why we use 20% of The Titans Group Annual Profit to develop and support global initiatives.

The Titans Group Foundation not only creates new initiatives but also supports other foundations like StandForEducation, Charlize Theron Africa Outreach Project, UNICEF, and Unesco.

To make a donation or become a volunteer please contact info@thetitansgroup.co
