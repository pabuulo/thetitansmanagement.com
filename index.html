Fashion

The Titans Management/TTG is an international management agency and mother agency managing models, stylists, photographers, and artists inside the fashion industry.

Our mission is to advocate for uniqueness and diversity within the entertainment industry by promoting and connecting our clients to worlwide brands and casting directors. The lack of diversity inside this business motivated us to create a new and innovative management method.

Submissions

The Titans Management/TTG is always on the look for new artists and new personalities.

Please Submit your pictures and a small bio to: submissions@thetitansmanagement.com

Literary

The Titans Literary works with international writers with the goal of achieving the publishing and high promotion of their work. The literary world is a sea of opportunities and experiences and we want to advocate and protect this ancient form of entertainment. The Titans Group Literary Department represents worldwide leading writers and screenplaywriters in the most varied genres.

Submissions

The Titans Literary is always looking for new manuscripts. If you would like to be considered for representation please submit your query to ONE of our agents at the time.
